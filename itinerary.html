Northern Italy 5-Day Itinerary

1 Day 1 – Arrival, Como & Transfer to Casasco d'Intelvi

  • • Arrival at Milan (Malpensa) around 11:00 AM. Pick up rental car and drive to Como (60–75 min).

  • • Como (Est. stay 4 hours):

    • – History: Ancient Roman settlement, later famed for silk production.
    • – Top 3:
      1. Duomo di Como: A stunning Gothic cathedral in the heart of the city. (10:30–17:00; free)
      2. Old Town stroll (cobbled alleys; 1–1.5h)
      3. Lakefront promenade (30–45 min)

  • Villa Olmo:

    A neoclassical villa from the 18th century with stunning interiors and gardens. Estimated time: 1 hour. Opening hours: 10:00–18:00 (closed on Mondays).

  • **Carrefour**:

    Buying groceries that will last till Thursday (Via Recchi 8, 8:00–21:00).

  • • Late afternoon:

    Drive to Villa Biber, Casasco d'Intelvi (~45 min).

2 Day 2 – Argegno, Pigra & Varenna

  • • Morning: Argegno (30 min drive):

    • – History: Medieval lakeside village, gateway to mountains.
    • – Top 3:
      1. Village center & bridge (30–45 min)
      2. Funivia Argegno–Pigra (8:30–12:00,14:00–17:30; ~€4)
      3. Scenic viewpoint at Pigra (30 min)
    • – Kosher: Small Carrefour Express (8:00–20:00).

  • • Early afternoon: Ferry to Varenna (~20 min; ~€4):

    • – History: Charming fishing village, villas & gardens.
    • – Top 3:
      1. Villa Monastero gardens (9:00–19:00; €11)
      2. Castello di Vezio ruins (10:00–17:00; €5)
      3. Lakeside promenade (30 min)

  • • Late afternoon:

    Return to Villa Biber.
  • * Note: Orrido di Bellano can take between 4 – 5.5h. It's cool but not essential unless you're specifically waterfall fans. Requires ferry logistics.

3 Day 3 – Zen Hiking in Val d'Intelvi + Spa

  • • Half Day : Zen hiking around Casasco d'Intelvi.

    • – Trail: Sentiero delle Espressioni (start/finish at Pian delle Alpi).
    • – Distance & Difficulty: 5 km circular, medium, 2.5–3h.
    • – Recommended: Start at 9:00 to enjoy morning light and cool air.
    • – If in the area: Picnic at clearings overlooking the valley.

  • • Spa + River chilling: #To Do -> To find places

4 Day 4 – Tremezzo, Menaggio & Villa Carlotta

  • • Morning: Drive to Tremezzo (40 min):

    • – History: Historic lakeside resort famed for grand villas.
    • – Top 3:
      1. Villa Carlotta (10:00–18:00; €13; book tickets in advance)
      2. Botanical gardens (1h)
      3. Waterfront promenade (30 min)

  • • Early afternoon: Menaggio (15 min drive):

    • – History: Former fishing village turned elegant resort.
    • – Top 3:
      1. Piazza Garibaldi (20 min)
      2. Menaggio Beach & promenade (30 min)
      3. Local artisans' shops (30 min)

  • • Late afternoon:

    Return to Villa Biber via scenic route.

5 Day 5 – Return to Milan & En Route Stops

  • • Check-out and drive toward Milan (2h15–2h45).

  • • Optional Stops:

    1. Monza Royal Villa & Gardens (9:00–18:00; €10)
    2. Bergamo Città Alta: Funicular (9:00–19:00; €2.30)
