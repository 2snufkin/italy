Itinerary

  • Settle into your accommodation
  • Explore Milan's historic center
  • Visit the Duomo di Milano
  • Take the funicular up to Brunate for panoramic views
  • Evening stroll along the lakefront promenade
  • Take a ferry to Bellagio (if not staying there)
  • Explore Villa Carlotta with its beautiful gardens
  • Visit the picturesque town of Varenna
  • Dinner in a lakeside restaurant
  • Visit the stunning Borromean Islands
  • Explore Isola Bella's baroque palace and gardens
  • Stop at Isola Madre for its exotic gardens
  • Return to Como in the evening
  • Take a short drive to Lake Lugano
  • Explore the charming city center
  • Take the funicular up Monte San Salvatore
  • Optional: visit the chocolate museum
  • Return to Como for dinner
  • Visit Villa del Balbianello (where Star Wars and James Bond were filmed)
  • Take a final boat trip on Lake Como
  • Shop in the silk stores of Como
  • Farewell dinner with lake views
