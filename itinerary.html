Northern Italy Trip Suggestions - May 18-23, 2025

Suggestion 1: Base City - Lake Como (Varenna)

Focuses on the Lake Como region, offering easy access to stunning scenery, hiking trails, charming villages, and a day trip to Switzerland. Perfect balance of outdoor activities with cultural experiences.

Key Features:

  • Centralized location in Varenna with good ferry connections
  • Extensive exploration of Lake Como area
  • Day trip to Switzerland (Lugano and Bellinzona)
  • Focus on hiking and outdoor activities

Suggestion 2: Base City - Varenna

A comprehensive plan centered in Varenna, featuring a mix of lake activities, Swiss exploration, and visits to multiple Italian lakes.

Key Features:

  • Exploration of Lake Como, Lake Orta, and Lake Maggiore
  • Visit to Lugano and Gandria in Switzerland
  • Mix of hiking and cultural activities
  • Emphasis on scenic locations and viewpoints

Suggestion 3: Base City - Bellagio

A balanced itinerary using Bellagio as the base, incorporating lake activities, mountain views, and Swiss culture.

Key Features:

  • Private boat tours of Lake Como
  • Visit to Lugano with Monte San Salvatore excursion
  • Exploration of Lake Maggiore
  • Combination of hiking and luxury experiences

Suggestion 4: Base City - Como

A detailed plan using Como as the central location, offering easy access to multiple lakes and Switzerland while maintaining a single home base.

Key Features:

  • Extensive exploration of Como city and surrounding areas
  • Day trip to Lugano, Switzerland
  • Visit to Lake Iseo and Monte Isola
  • Balance of urban comforts and outdoor adventures

Common Elements Across All Suggestions:

  • Car rental from Milan Malpensa Airport
  • Single base location to minimize hotel changes
  • Mix of outdoor activities and cultural experiences
  • Day trip to Switzerland
  • Focus on lakeside locations and scenic views
  • Combination of hiking opportunities and town exploration
